Binomial Pricing Model

The Binomial Pricing Model is a discrete-time model for valuing options. In the attached paper, I aim to articulate the mathematical underpinnings of an Excel-based tool I developed for valuing a range of derivatives, such as call and put options, zero coupon bonds, defaultable bonds, caplets, and floorlets, building on foundational courses in financial engineering.
